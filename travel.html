Travel & Getting There

We are so excited to celebrate our special days with you in Shivamogga and Davanagere! To help you plan your journey smoothly, here are a few travel options and tips.

🌸 Reception, Wedding — Shivamogga (June 24 and 25, 2026)

Arya Edigara Bhavana, Shivamogga

Open in Maps

✈️ BY AIR

The nearest airports to Shivamogga are:

  • • Shivamogga Airport (RQY)
  • • Bangalore International Airport (BLR) – approx 300 km away

From either airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Shivamogga.

🚆 BY TRAIN

The nearest railway station is:

  • • Shivamogga Town Railway Station (SMET)

Shivamogga is well-connected by train to major cities like Bengaluru and Mysuru.

🚗 BY ROAD

  • • Regular KSRTC and private buses are available
  • • You can also hire a cab or drive down
  • • From Bengaluru, travel time is approx 6–7 hours

🚖 GETTING AROUND

  • • Auto-rickshaws and taxis are easily available
  • • App-based services like Rapido, NammaYatri, Ola and Uber can be used in most areas

🎉 Reception — Davanagere (June 28, 2026)

Shamanur Shivashankarappa Parvathamma Samudhaya Bhavana
Hadadi Road, Davanagere

Open in Maps

✈️ BY AIR

The nearest airport to Davanagere is:

  • • Kempegowda Intl Airport – approx 265 km away

From the airport, taxis and buses are available to reach Davanagere.

🚆 BY TRAIN

The nearest railway station is:

  • • Davanagere Railway Station (DVG)

Davanagere is well-connected by train to Bengaluru, Hubballi, and other major cities.

🚗 BY ROAD

  • • Well connected via NH-48
  • • Regular KSRTC and private buses operate frequently
  • • From Bengaluru, travel time is approx 5–6 hours

🚖 GETTING AROUND

  • • Auto-rickshaws and taxis are widely available
  • • App-based cab services like Ola and Uber are accessible in most areas

📅 Travel Tips

  • • We recommend arriving by June 24, 2026 for the wedding celebrations in Shivamogga
  • • If attending both events, you can plan travel between Shivamogga and Davanagere (approx. 2 hours by road)
  • • June is monsoon season, do carry an umbrella
  • • Please allow buffer time for travel, especially by road
  • • For any assistance during your journey, feel free to reach out to us!
